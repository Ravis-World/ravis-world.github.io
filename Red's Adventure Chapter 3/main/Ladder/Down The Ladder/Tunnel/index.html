Tunnel

The birds proceed down a narrow tunnel carved with ancient symbols. The markings run in a continuous line — but unlike modern scripts, they flow bottom to top.

Chuck pauses, his eyes scanning rapidly. “Wait... I recognise this! It’s Ogham — old Irish script.”

Matilda raises a brow. “How do you even know that?”
Chuck: “I read fast. Like, really fast.”

The symbols appear below, carved into the tunnel wall:




᚛ᚐᚔᚅᚋᚆᚔ᚜᚛ᚑᚚᚐᚏ᚜᚛ᚃᚓᚖᚂ᚜



After a moment, Chuck translates: “animal. job. meat.”

Red frowns. “That’s… unsettlingly vague.”
Bomb: “Maybe it’s a menu?”
Blue gulps. “Are we the meat?”
Up across the birds see a computer lock. They have to enter the passcode based on those three words. Maybe they point to something that is related.
