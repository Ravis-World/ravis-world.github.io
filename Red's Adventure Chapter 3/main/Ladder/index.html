Ladder

Red squints at the rust-covered ladder. “Think this thing still works?”
Matilda inspects the bolts. “Mostly... but we'll have to climb quietly. One wrong step and it might collapse.”
One by one, the birds descend carefully. Bomb holds his breath the entire time.
“I'm not sure I like this,” he whispers, watching the ladder sway slightly with each movement.
“Just keep your balance,” Matilda reassures him. “We'll be fine.”
