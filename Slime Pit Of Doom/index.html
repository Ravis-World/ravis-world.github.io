The Boots Room - Job Rankings

The workers are assigned their footwear (with sizes to fit all) based on rank and experience. When you work, your boots determine how well you can navigate the **Slime Pit of Doom**, a hazardous but thrilling environment filled with various sticky, corrosive, and sentient substances, while mainly defining your skill level; what type you are. Only the best workers earn the right of the elite.

What is the Slime Pit of Doom?

The Slime Pit of Doom is a vast, ever-changing terrain filled with all kinds of goo, adhesive substances, and dangerous liquids. You must navigate different pits, from harmless mud and honey to stinging alien goo. Higher-ranked workers gain access to boots that resist even the most powerful substances.

Low Ranks (Beginner Workers)

White Gladiator Sandals

Entry-level footwear with no real protection. Slime seeps through easily.

Yellow Ballet Shoes

Better than sandals, but still absorb slime easily, making them slippery.

Orange Sneakers

Sneakers offering basic pit resistance, but not suited for deep goo.

Mid Ranks (Experienced Workers)

Green Leather Boots

Durable and flexible, great for navigating sticky and tar-like pits.

Blue Rubber Boots

Rubber boots resistant to stronger adhesives like glue pits.

Purple High-Waders

Hip-high boots designed for deep pits, preventing overflow.

High Ranks (Elite Workers/Managers)

Brown Heavy-Duty Leather Boots

Ultra-durable and resistant to heat, corrosion, and hive muck.

Red Reinforced Rubber Boots

Fireproof and acid-resistant, designed to handle alien goo safely.

Black Tactical Boots

The highest-tier elite footwear, nearly immune to all pit effects.

Legendary Rank

Gold Knight Boots

Crafted from enchanted gold, reinforced with mystical alloys. Symbol of ultimate protection and prestige.

The Pinnacle of Power

Manager’s Sovereign Boots

Forged from meteorite metal and platinum, these boots give the Manager complete control over all pits.
