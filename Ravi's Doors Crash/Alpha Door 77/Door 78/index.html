Door 78 – Memory Mismatch
CR α

After escaping the security room, the team found themselves in another corridor. The corridor was filled with portraits of the team… but they're wrong —

  • Door is missing
  • Raviolo has a corrupted face
  • Grey Box is painted crying
  • Diamond is an actual diamond
  • Stella never met Raviolo and lived a normal life in her house
  • Toadette is recreated in Plotagon

The team was confused. They had just escaped the security room, but now they were faced with a new challenge. The portraits on the walls seemed to mock them, each one a twisted version of their own faces. It was as if someone had taken their memories and distorted them into something grotesque.
"What is this place?" asked Raviolo, looking at the portraits with a mix of fear and anger. "Why are our faces all wrong?"
"I don't know," replied Door, her voice shaky. "But we need to find a way out of here. This place is messing with our heads."
The team pressed on, their hearts racing as they navigated the twisted corridor. They could feel the weight of the portraits bearing down on them, each one a reminder of their own fears and insecurities. It was as if the very walls were alive, whispering secrets and lies that threatened to tear them apart.
We need to stick together, we can't let this place get to us. We have to stay focused and find a way out.
Objective: Rearrange the pieces to complete the puzzle or risk getting trapped in a false memory loop.
