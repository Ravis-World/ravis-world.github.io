According to the table, the team have landed in Mokha, on the planet of Mǒchá; this world is known for its famous intergalactic tea, and that's why Raviolo built all these portals: to fast-travel to his favourite hotspots. The catch is he uses the gemstones to power the portals up.
The birds ask the locals about the location of the yellow gemstone; the one that swapped places with the green gemstone. They are more than happy to show you the way, but they are warned that it is near impossible to get due to power surges in the facility and outside of the building.
Citizen: Be careful of the power surges; we don't know where they came from.
