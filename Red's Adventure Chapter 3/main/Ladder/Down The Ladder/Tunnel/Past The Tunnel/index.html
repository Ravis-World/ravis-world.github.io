Showdown

As the door unlocks with a hiss, the birds step into a massive chamber lined with flickering monitors. Thick cables snake across the floor, converging at a single figure standing in the centre of the room.

It’s Creeper Jack.

He turns slowly, eyes glowing with a strange green light.

Creeper Jack: “You’ve done well... too well. But this is where your adventure ends.”
Bomb growls. “We’ll see about that.”
Matilda: “Everyone ready? He’s not holding back.”
